About Gratis Library

The Web development community is a very inclusive group; always building fresh resources to help teach the next generation all the latest languages, frameworks, and cutting edge techniques. Gratis Library is a curated list that specializes in the free eBooks and podcasts you can read and listen to on the go to improve your skillset.

All of the eBooks in the library have been made available from the author without cost. A few require signing up for a newsletter or sending a tweet or something before you can access the PDF though these are clearly identified as such. Some eBooks come in different formats like online only (HTML), PDF, ePub, etc. The podcasts are free both to stream and download. Some have RSS or iTunes feeds covering their entire catalog. Several of the podcasts have been archived (discontinued), however the content is still considered relevant or interesting so they have been included in the list. Both eBooks and Podcasts provide a link to the contents homepage and hope that you support the author.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I add a missing eBook or Podcast missing to your catalog?
Currently, the easiest way to contribute is to email your submission and I'll review the title to ensure it meets the quality standards for the site. Remember: it can't be spam-like, it must be truely free, and it should be worth reading or listening too!
Are you planning to add another source of free useful content?
At the moment, I'm sticking to free eBooks and Podcasts as that's what I tend to read and listen to myself on a regular basis; it keeps me up-to-date and learn interesting new viewpoints on industry subjects. Maybe in the future I'll revisit this subject if I get time.
How often do you add new eBooks and Podcasts to the list?
I plan to roll out updates whenever I come across fresh titles on the Web. With the initial release of the app I already have quite an extensive collection of both eBooks (750+) and Podcasts (100+), so you'll find a wealth of content already available for you to enjoy.
